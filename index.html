NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token." It is a type of digital asset that represents ownership or proof of authenticity of a unique item or piece of content using blockchain technology. Unlike fungible tokens such as cryptocurrencies (e.g., Bitcoin or Ethereum), which are interchangeable with each other and have the same value, NFTs are unique and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis.
